### 2. The Paradox of Cleanliness
The most significant discovery was the **Success Paradox**. 
In Oekolopoly, a "perfect" environment (Environmental Damage < 10) triggers a massive bonus to Quality of Life (QoL). This leads to exponential QoL growth which eventually exceeds the simulation's hard threshold (QoL > 35), causing immediate systemic collapse. 

**Conclusion:** To survive, the Governor must maintain a "controlled level of filth" (Environmental Damage between 12 and 18) to suppress catastrophic QoL bonuses.

### 3. The Alchemist Burn Strategy
The simulation forces the dissipation of all Action Points (AP). Accumulating too many points or failing to spend them leads to death. The Sovereign Champion uses a tiered "AP Sink" priority list:
1.  **Strategic Sabotage:** Intentionally lowering Production. This burns AP, reduces QoL growth, and prevents the environment from becoming "too clean."
2.  **Educational Satiation:** Maxing out Education (Level 29) to ensure a stable AP income of ~25-30 per round.
3.  **Cybernetic Population Control:** Maintaining population between 18 and 32 units to avoid resource depletion.

*   **Neural Layer (RecurrentPPO):** Suggests high-level strategic directions based on a long-short-term memory (LSTM) of the simulation state.
*   **Symbolic Layer (SovereignGuardian):** Intercepts the neural actions and applies deterministic, budget-aware constraints. It acts as the "Oracle" that prevents the agent from making fatal mistakes.

The **V106 Environmental Alchemist** represents the pinnacle of Oekolopoly governance. By intentionally balancing pollution and production, it creates a stable, immortal equilibrium. This model serves as the definitive solution to the Oekolopoly-v2 simulation.
